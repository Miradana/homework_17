About Me

Nayeli Miranda

I am a visual artist from Austin, Texas. While searching for a new career path I encountered some information online about UX/UI Design, I decided to look more in depth into it and find out more about it. It caught my attention because it combines various disciplines such as design and technology, so I decided that it was a career that I want to pursue.

    Trippee, Your Travel Companion

    When people take a vacation, they want to have the best experience possible. Booking hotels, flights, things to do, and keeping organized can be stressful and a hassle.

    The Problem: Travel planning isn’t perfect travel websites are not work as smoothly as users wish they did. It can be overwhelming with all the oversaling out there.

    The Solution: A travel planning website that allows you to compare and book flights and accommodation and share it with friends in real time

    Keep an Itinerary

    Trippee can help you find things to do, save them in a list view, and organized them on a timeline.

    On the map view you can organize your itinerary in terms of distance an location
